Why Choose Us?

Our farms are located in the Netherlands and California and we are one of the largest flower growers and exporters. We sell top quality flowers in different varieties: tulips, daffodils, hyacints, dahlias and much more.

The Netherlands produces 1.7 billion cut flowers per year which represents about 50% of the global trade.

Unsurpassed Quality

The Netherlands (Holland) is known as the "Flower Capital of the World" and we produce high quality flower bulbs and cut flowers, with rigorous inspection processes and lab examinations, focusing also on innovation and sustainability ensuring a globally recognized standard.

We guarantee the longevity of our flowers during long-distance transport.
